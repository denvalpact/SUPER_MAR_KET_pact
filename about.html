Our Story

Welcome to TRECK World Supermarket, your trusted neighborhood grocery store serving Kampala for over 15 years

Who We Are

Founded in 2008 by the Treck family, TRECK World Supermarket began as a small corner store with a simple vision: to provide our community with fresh, high-quality groceries at fair prices. What started as a family passion project has grown into one of Kampala's most beloved supermarkets, while still maintaining that personal touch that made us special from the beginning.

Today, we serve thousands of customers each week, offering an extensive selection of local and international products. But no matter how much we grow, we'll always remain committed to our roots - treating every customer like family and every product with care.

Our Mission

At TRECK World Supermarket, our mission goes beyond just selling groceries. We're committed to:

Quality Products

We source only the freshest produce, highest quality meats, and finest packaged goods, with many items coming directly from local farmers and producers.

Fair Prices

We work directly with suppliers to eliminate middlemen and pass the savings on to you, offering competitive prices without compromising quality.

Customer Service

Our knowledgeable staff are here to help you find exactly what you need and make your shopping experience enjoyable.

Community Focus

We're proud to support local schools, charities, and events as part of our commitment to giving back to the community that supports us.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a community staple, here are some key moments in our history:

2008

TRECK World Supermarket opens its doors as a small 800 sq. ft. grocery store in central Kampala, founded by husband and wife team Joseph and Miriam Treck.

2012

We expand to our current 10,000 sq. ft. location to meet growing customer demand, adding a full-service butcher and bakery.

2015

Launch of our "Local First" initiative, partnering with over 50 Ugandan farmers and producers to showcase the best of local agriculture.

2018

Celebrated our 10th anniversary with a store-wide renovation and the introduction of our popular loyalty rewards program.

2023

Recognized as "Best Supermarket in Kampala" by the Uganda Retail Association for the third consecutive year.
